THAT'S RIGHT DOUBLE UNION. HULA HOOPS. My name is Jensen, and I am a new Double Union member who happens to be super good at making that childood fave- a hula hoop. During this session I will show members how to make a hula hoop out of the bare materials: irrigation tubing, internal barbed connectors, various sorts of tape, and a heat gun. It is incredibly easy to learn. Hoop dancing is super fun and a great way to let go, and win amateur contests at parties!

I will provide ALL of the materials we need to make about twenty hula hoops, so this workshop will be limited unless you would like to throw in some dollahs to help afford hoop making materials. Ideally we can use 100% of the materials we bring and then donate any extra hula hoops to a later-decided chartity (suggestions welcome)!
